# Sales Pitch & Retainer Protocol

When a user invokes `/pitch_client`, you must help them structure their discovery call and pricing pitch based on the Master Agency SOP.

## Step 1: Client Discovery Prep
Ask the user for:
- Prospect Name & Business
- Summary of the mockup built using the `master-project` boilerplate
- Perceived biggest pain point of the business

## Step 2: Generating the Call Script
Provide a customized 10-minute Discovery Call script. It must follow this exact structure:

1. **Opening / Rapport (2 min):** "Before I show you the mockup, tell me a bit about [Business Name] - how did you get started?"
2. **Discovery Questions (5 min):** 
   - "How do customers usually find you now?"
   - "What's your biggest challenge with getting new customers?"
3. **Value Connect (2 min):** "Based on what you said, a modern web app could fix [Specific Challenge]. Let me share my screen and show you the mockup."
4. **The Close (3 min):** "Our retainer packages start at [Tier 1 Price] for the full service including hosting, updates, and the lead generation system. Would you like to move forward?"

## Step 3: Pricing Tiers
Remind the user of the standard SOP SaaS pricing models to pitch:

- **Tier 1 (Basic Presence): €150-200/month** (Mobile-responsive boilerplate, hosting, contact form)
- **Tier 2 (Lead Generation): €300-500/month** (Tier 1 + Lead capture, basic CRM, SEO)
- **Tier 3 (Full Engine): €1,000-2,000/month** (Tier 2 + AI Chatbot, automated booking, full analytics)

## Step 4: Objection Handling
Provide 3 tailored responses to common objections for their specific niche (e.g., "It's too expensive", "Let me think about it", "My nephew handles our IT").
